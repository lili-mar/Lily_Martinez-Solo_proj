CommunEty is a local service derived from the concept of the gift economy


where neighbors help Each other.

A gift economy nurtures the communEty bonds which enhance mutual well-being; the economic unit is Everyone("we") rather than "I," as all flourishing is mutual.
CommunEty's currency is based on gratitude and reciprocity not monetary value. This local service will allow CommunEty's members to do their own trading without any explicit expectations of direct compensation of any goods or services exchanged.
