Your pledge to the CommunEty


1. Be respectful to your neighbors

You are speaking to your real neighbors. Strong communities are built on strong relationships.

2. Do not discriminate

Help build a society that does not tolerate racism, hateful language, or discrimination of any kind.

3. Discuss important topics in a safe and respectful way

We have policies and dedicated spaces for important non-local topics, such as national politics.

4. Promote your local gift economy

We have created designated spaces for members and local businesses to share products and services.

5. Use your true identity

CommunEty is built on trust — we want everyone to know they’re communicating with their real neighbor, and therefore require you to use your true identity.

6. Do not engage in harmful activity

We prohibit any activity that could hurt someone, from physical harm to scams.


You, our members who make up this CommunEty, play a key role in neighborhood moderation by reporting content or members that violate these Guidelines. Please remember that disagreeing with a post is not a reason to report it.
Our CommunEty pledge to you is to create an environment where Everyone feels welcome.
