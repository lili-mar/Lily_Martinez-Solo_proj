Example of how the CommunEty works

Back to Login

1. Jim's lawn mower won't start, and he is hoping to mow the lawn before his in-laws come for dinner tonight.

2. Jim goes to communEty.com and since he already has an account, he logs in and checks out that he is “looking for something.”

3. Jim types in a message with his immediate need to borrow a lawnmover.

4. Gary is already in the communEty site, and reads Jim's new request.

5. Gary comments on Jim's new message that Gary is happy to help.

6. Jim provides Gary a way to directly communicate in a safe manner.

7. Jim really appreciates being part of the communEty and his in-laws compliment him on his beautiful yard!

8. Gary, without any expectations, shared his abundance today!


NOTE: The same process applies for "sharing something"
